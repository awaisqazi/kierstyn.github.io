Who I Am

Writing Songs has been a go to habit for Kierstyn Alysse since her mother removed her TV from her room in 2nd grade. She transferred boredom into creativity by penning hundreds of songs. Influenced by songwriters such as NIja Charles and The-Dream, Kierstyn found her muse in pop inspired R&B songs. Although she envisions herself as a songwriter first and foremost, Kierstyn began her journey as an artist and eventually has plans to take her artistry to the next level.

My Work

A cool R&B song with unforgettable melodies and relatable lyrics is exactly what music lovers look for in a song and ‘Memories’ is just that! The song highlights a relatable topic that many people can say they’ve experienced if they’ve ever engaged in dating life. It is ultimately an anthem for women who are involved with a partner that isn’t giving their all in a relationship.
